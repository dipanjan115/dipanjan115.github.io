About Me

I am a PhD student at the Performance and Resource Optimization in Networks (PROTON) Lab at the University of New Mexico under the guidance of Dr. Eirni Eleni Tsiropoulou. I completed my bachelor of science in electrical engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in January 2019. My research focus was on green communication. In 2023 I complted my master's in communication technology from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) with a specialization in information security. I have worked with Nordic Semiconductor in Torndheim Norway for my master's thesis focusing on QoS improvement in Bluetooh mesh networks. My research interst lies in resource management in modern communication systems, QoS improvement, reinforcement learning, embedded systems, internet of things (IoT) and information security.
